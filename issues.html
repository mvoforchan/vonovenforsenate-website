Issues & Priorities

Common sense solutions for the challenges facing District 48 families

District 48 deserves a State Senator who focuses on solving real problems, not scoring political points. Mark's priorities are shaped by his experience on City Council, his work with families across our communities, and data-driven analysis of what actually works.

Affordability & Fiscal Responsibility

Families in District 48 are feeling squeezed: housing costs are rising, property taxes keep climbing, and everyday expenses eat into family budgets. Mark believes in fiscally responsible government that keeps costs down while making smart investments in our future.

Mark's Plan:

  • Control Property Taxes: Oppose unfunded mandates that force local tax increases
  • Housing Affordability: Support policies that increase housing supply and make homeownership accessible
  • Smart Investments: Fund priorities that deliver value: education, infrastructure, public safety
  • Transparent Budgeting: Make state budget impacts clear and understandable for taxpayers
  • Small Business Support: Reduce regulatory burdens and support local job creators

Education

Our children deserve excellent schools with well-supported teachers, modern facilities, and programs that prepare them for the future. Mark believes fully funding education is an investment in Minnesota's future, not an expense to cut.

Mark's Plan:

  • Full Funding: Adequate per-pupil funding that keeps pace with actual costs
  • Teacher Support: Competitive salaries, professional development, and classroom resources
  • Mental Health: Counselors, social workers, and support systems for student wellbeing
  • Early Childhood: Expand access to quality pre-K and early learning programs
  • Safe Schools: Modern, well-maintained facilities where kids can learn and thrive
  • Local Control: Trust educators and school boards to make decisions for their communities

Constitutional Rights & Transparency

Mark stood up for constitutional principles during an ICE incident in Chanhassen in subzero temperatures, when no one else did. He believes government must operate transparently and respect civil liberties for everyone, regardless of politics or partisan pressure.

Mark's Plan:

  • Due Process for All: Protect constitutional rights regardless of background or beliefs
  • Open Government: Make state operations transparent and accessible to citizens
  • Privacy Rights: Protect Minnesotans from unreasonable searches and government overreach
  • Local Control: Respect proper division of powers between federal, state, and local government
  • Accountability: Hold officials and agencies responsible at every level

Environment & Water Quality

Our lakes, parks, and natural spaces define District 48's quality of life. Mark believes we have a responsibility to preserve these treasures for future generations while supporting smart, sustainable economic growth.

Mark's Plan:

  • Clean Water: Protect our lakes from pollution and invest in water quality monitoring
  • Parks & Trails: Maintain and expand outdoor recreational opportunities
  • Sustainable Growth: Balance development with environmental protection
  • Climate Action: Support clean energy while maintaining affordability
  • Conservation: Preserve natural areas and wildlife habitats

Public Safety

Safe communities require supporting law enforcement while also investing in prevention, mental health response, and addressing root causes of crime. Mark believes in smart, comprehensive approaches to public safety.

Mark's Plan:

  • Support First Responders: Adequate funding, equipment, and training for police and fire
  • Mental Health Response: Crisis intervention teams and mental health professionals
  • Community Policing: Build trust and relationships between law enforcement and residents
  • Prevention Programs: Youth programs, addiction treatment, and addressing root causes
  • Emergency Preparedness: Invest in disaster response and public health infrastructure
  • Accountability: Professional standards and transparency in law enforcement
