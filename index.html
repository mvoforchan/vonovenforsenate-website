Thank You

On Saturday March 21st, the DFL delegates of Senate District 48 made their choice. While it wasn't the outcome we hoped for, I couldn't be more proud of what we built together.

Thank you to everyone who believed in this campaign — the donors who made it possible, the volunteers who gave their time, the delegates who fought for fresh leadership, and my family who supported me every step of the way.

I will continue to serve the residents of Chanhassen as your City Councilmember, doing the work you elected me to do.

This campaign may have ended, but my commitment to this community and these principles hasn't changed one bit.

With gratitude,
Mark von Oven
