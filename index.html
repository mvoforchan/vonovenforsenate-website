Why I'm Running

District 48 deserves a State Senator who brings people together to solve real problems, not someone who divides us for political gain. As a City Councilmember, I've proven I can find common ground, listen to all voices, and deliver results that make our communities stronger.

Whether it's managing budgets responsibly, investing in our schools, or standing up for constitutional principles when it matters most, I'm committed to leadership that puts people before politics.

Our community deserves a State Senator who will:

  • Keep government accountable and transparent
  • Protect constitutional rights for everyone
  • Make decisions based on data and common sense
  • Put people before politics
  • Deliver results that improve our daily lives
